Informatik 3 Exercise 2


Partners Agency
Date Issued
13 December 01
Date Due
10 January 02
The objective of the exercise is to write a Prolog program that could be used as the basis for a Partners Agency to match lists of men and women seeking partners.
Tasks
Step 1: Data Input
  1. Enter a list of the names of men seeking partners.
  2. Enter a list of the names of women seeking partners. The names are separated by blancs and the list of names is terminated by newline. There should be the same number of men as women in the partnering process.
  3. For each person, enter the city that person lives in.
  4. For each person, enter a list of at most 5 interests e.g. music, films, cooking. The interests are separated by blancs and the list of interests is terminated by newline.
  5. Each man should indicate for each woman how attractive they find that woman by giving a number in the range 1-10. (10 = most attractive)
  6. Each woman should indicate for each man how attractive they find that man by giving a number in the range 1-10. (10 = most attractive)
Step 2: Generate Preference Lists
For each person, generate a list indicating their partner preferences assuming
  1. The primary factor is level of attractiveness.
  2. The secondary factor is the number of interests that they have in common.
  3. The third factor is the same city.
Step 3: Find a Best Match of Partners
Based on the preference lists generated in step 2, find a best match of partners such that there are no pairs of men and women who would prefer to be with each other rather than with their partners.
Step 4: Output the Results
Generate a table showing the recommended partners.
